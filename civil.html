Civil Engineering Experience

Lever Australia Dec 2010 - Dec 2013

  • Jan 2016

Whilst studying my Bachelor of Engineering (Civil) at the University of Queensland, I was employed as an undergraduate for Lever Australia. During the three (3) years of work experience, I was involved in many projects including conceptual stages, design, drawing and site supervision. Below is a brief description of my roles in the project design.

Notable Projects

Lake Vermont Site Expansion and Flood Study

Architectural design and draft of maintenance and site offices, slab design and fitout for light vehicles washpad, heavy vehicle washpad, fuel farm and central maintenance workshop area. Laid out site plan on aerial image for traffic requirements. Design and draft of pipeline, dam expansion and manifold redesign for environmental dam.

Lihir Gold Infrastructure Upgrade

Layout and drafting for lubricant, crusher and ancillary workshops, assisting in column and slab design. Design of walkways and access stairs to crusher including design of counterweighted personnel drawbridge. Layout, slab design and drafting of effluent pit, bunds and light tower footings.

Stanmore Train Loadout

3D model design of train rail using LiDAR data and Autodesk Civil 3D, along with design and draft of loadout using Autodesk Inventor for 3D rendering.

Lihir Gold Dome Roof Maintenance Area

Design, floor plans and drafting of shipping container maintenance area with dome roof, along with fitout including container connections, office and bathroom design for short term use. This included assisting and proofreading the cost estimate.

Yarrabee Coal Mine Infrastructure Area

Architectural design and draft of eight bay workshop, single bay tyre change area and washpad (including fit-out) with relevant site layouts. Design of slab plans and details, stairway design and assisted design of workshop columns.

Software

AutoCAD Mechanical

Essential CAD software used in all designs for 2D layouts, detailed drawings and single plane representations of design. Utilised in almost all projects.

AutoDesk Inventor

3D rendering software used for architectural drawings in most projects including washpads, workshops, on-site offices, pipe manifolds and light towers.

Civil 3D

CAD design software with in-built features used heavily to design access roads, carparks, train loadout and 3D site layouts for drainage.

SpaceGass

Multi-purpose 3D analysis program used to obtain loadings for column, beam and light tower designs.
